The Divide from Denominations
Denominations have a lot of things good about them, but in their nature, they cause divide. Every Christian is united as brothers and sisters but their beliefs lead to separation in the religion. I would like to meantion, I do think that we need denominations as it is very tough to believe all the same ideas, but as long as we are believing Jesus Christ is Lord, then most of the other ideas are not as fundamental (unless it's about the trinity, looking at JW's and Mormons).
Why Christianity?
Out of every single other religion, why is Christianity the only correct one? Google says that there is from 2500-16000 gods (depending on the source), but yet we only believe one. To unbelieves, this could be hard to grasp that we don't believe in the other 15999 or so, and I have heard this used as an anrgument agaisnt Christianity. I believe I can try to help explain why this is, and why it is the only thing that is logically correct.
